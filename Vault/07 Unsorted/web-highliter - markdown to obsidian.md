## Markdown Export

### What can this integration do?

Effortlessly send your highlights, notes, and tags to Obsidian, Notion, Capacities, and more using the copy to markdown feature. [Learn more](https://web-highlights.com/blog/how-to-export-your-research-to-obsidian-with-web-highlights/).

### How it works

1. 
    Copy highlights & notes to markdown
    
    ![How to copy to markdown](../03%20-%20MEDIA%20&%20FILES/c47cc9e691c174e17d7ecd0066127393_MD5.png)
    
    First, you can go to any highlighted page either from the [web app](https://app.web-highlights.com/) or the extension. 
	
	Then click the three dots in the top right corner and select '**Copy Markdown**'.



2. 
    Paste your MarkdownThen, go [Notion](https://notion.so/?ref=https://app.web-highlights.com), [Obsidian](https://obsidian.md/?ref=https://app.web-highlights.com), [Capacities](https://app.capacities.io/?ref=https://app.web-highlights.com), or any other service and paste the markdown to the page. Once you've pasted the content onto the page, you should see your highlights, notes, and tags. If your highlighted page has a banner image, that should be visible as well.
